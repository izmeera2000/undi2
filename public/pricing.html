Frequently Asked Questions
Can I change my plan at any time?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference immediately. When downgrading, the new rate will apply at your next billing cycle.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise plans. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.
Is there a free trial available?
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial for the Pro plan. No credit card required. You can explore all Pro features during the trial period and decide if it's right for you.
What happens when I exceed my storage limit?
You'll receive a notification when you reach 80% of your storage. If you exceed your limit, you can either upgrade your plan or purchase additional storage at $0.50/GB per month.
Do you offer discounts for nonprofits?
Yes, we offer a 50% discount for verified nonprofits and educational institutions. Contact our sales team with proof of your organization's status to apply for the discount.
