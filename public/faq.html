What is ModernAdmin and what can I do with it?

ModernAdmin is a comprehensive admin dashboard solution designed to help businesses manage their operations efficiently. With ModernAdmin, you can:

  • Track key business metrics and analytics in real-time
  • Manage users, teams, and permissions
  • Handle projects and tasks with built-in collaboration tools
  • Generate reports and export data in multiple formats
  • Integrate with your favorite tools via our API
How do I create my first project?

Creating a project is simple:

  1. Navigate to the Projects section from the sidebar
  2. Click the "New Project" button in the top right
  3. Fill in your project details (name, description, team members)
  4. Choose a template or start from scratch
  5. Click "Create Project" to finish

Your new project will appear in your dashboard immediately.

Can I invite team members to my workspace?

Yes! You can invite unlimited team members on paid plans. To invite someone:

  1. Go to Settings → Team
  2. Click "Invite Member"
  3. Enter their email address and select a role
  4. They'll receive an invitation email to join your workspace

Team members can have different roles: Admin, Editor, or Viewer.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes! We offer native mobile apps for both iOS and Android. You can download them from:

App Store Google Play

The mobile app includes notifications, quick actions, and access to all essential features.

Account & Profile
6 articles
How do I change my password?

To change your password:

  1. Go to Settings → Security
  2. Click "Change Password"
  3. Enter your current password, then your new password twice
  4. Click "Update Password"

For security, you'll be logged out of other devices after changing your password.

How can I update my profile information?

Your profile information can be updated at any time:

  1. Navigate to Settings → Profile
  2. Update your name, email, phone, or profile picture
  3. Changes are saved automatically

Note: Changing your email requires verification of the new address.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can request account deletion from Settings → Account → Delete Account.

Warning: This action is irreversible. All your data will be permanently deleted after a 30-day grace period. Download a backup of your data before proceeding.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

Visa Mastercard Amex PayPal

Enterprise customers can also pay via bank transfer or invoice.

How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

You can change your plan at any time:

  1. Go to Settings → Billing → Change Plan
  2. Select your new plan
  3. Confirm the change

Upgrades: You'll be charged the prorated difference immediately.

Downgrades: The new rate applies at your next billing cycle.

Can I get a refund?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days, contact our support team for a full refund—no questions asked.

After 30 days, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis.

Where can I find my invoices?

All invoices are available in Settings → Billing → Invoice History. You can:

  • View invoice details
  • Download as PDF
  • Print directly from the browser

Invoices are also sent to your billing email automatically after each payment.

How do I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)?

To enable 2FA:

  1. Go to Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication
  2. Click "Enable 2FA"
  3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
  4. Enter the 6-digit code to verify
  5. Save your backup codes in a secure location
Tip: We recommend using an authenticator app over SMS for better security.
Is my data encrypted?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption:

  • In transit: TLS 1.3 encryption for all data transfers
  • At rest: AES-256 encryption for stored data
  • Passwords: Bcrypt hashing with salt

We also conduct regular security audits and penetration testing.

What should I do if I suspect unauthorized access?

If you suspect unauthorized access:

  1. Change your password immediately
  2. Enable 2FA if not already enabled
  3. Review active sessions in Settings → Security → Active Sessions
  4. Terminate any suspicious sessions
  5. Contact our security team at [email protected]

Yes! We provide a comprehensive REST API for Pro and Enterprise plans. With our API, you can:

  • Access and modify your data programmatically
  • Build custom integrations
  • Automate workflows
  • Create custom dashboards

How do I export my data?

You can export your data anytime from Settings → Data → Export. Supported formats:

  • CSV - For spreadsheet applications
  • JSON - For developers and integrations
  • PDF - For reports and documentation

Large exports are processed in the background, and you'll receive a download link via email.
